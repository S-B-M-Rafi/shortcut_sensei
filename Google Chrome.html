Note on Keyboard Combinations:

A plus (+) signifies that you should press those keys simultaneously. A comma (,) signifies that you should press keys sequentially. For instance, "Ctrl+N" indicates holding down the Ctrl key while pressing the N key and then releasing both keys. Conversely, "Alt+N,P" entails holding down the Alt key, pressing the N key, releasing the N key, pressing the P key, and then releasing all keys.
